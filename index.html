HAVE QUESTIONS? WE HAVE ANSWERS.

Ease into the world of StyleLoom with clarity. Our FAQs cover a spectrum of topics.

How can I place an order on StyleLoom?

Browse the site, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your details and complete your purchase.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Once an order is confirmed, modifications or cancellations may not be possible. Please review your order carefully before completing the purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, and select digital wallets. Choose the option that suits you best during checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Use it to follow your package in real time on our website.

How do I initiate a return?

Visit our Returns page and follow the provided instructions. Ensure your item meets the return criteria, and our team will guide you through the process.

Do you offer exchanges for products?

We don't offer direct exchanges right now. If you'd like a different item, please initiate a return and place a new order.
